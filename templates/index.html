Student Visa

A student visa is a travel authorization issued by countries around the world. It permits foreign students to study at a university or college within its national territory.

Business Visa

A business visa is a travel authorization that allows an entrepreneur or company employees to visit a foreign country temporarily for business purposes.

Family Visa

A family visa is a type of visa that legally permits foreign nationals to live with their relatives in another country.
